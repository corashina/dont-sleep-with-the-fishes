## Project-authored item models

They are not third-party assets and intentionally have no rows in the third-party table: `project-item-models@1:compass` (88 triangles), `project-item-models@1:map` (80), `project-item-models@1:spyglass` (160), `project-item-models@1:fishingNet` (416), `project-item-models@1:flareGun` (292), `project-item-models@1:anchor` (292), `project-item-models@1:umbrella` (268), `project-item-models@1:swimRing` (240), `project-item-models@1:harpoonGun` (108), and `project-item-models@1:energyBar` (48).
